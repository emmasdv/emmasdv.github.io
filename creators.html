Casidy Cunningham

Casidy Cunningham (she/her) is a rising junior and Human Rights major at Columbia University.

As a creative outlet, Cas uses her free time to crochet! So far, she’s made sweaters, a wrap top, a cardigan, a bucket hat, shorts, and bralettes—including a bralette she completely designed herself. She first learned in 6th grade and picked it back up in December after being inspired by another Black crochet artist, @Eurythmik on IG and Twitter.

As a low-income student, Cas has been accepting custom orders as a small source of income. However, she has also been able to donate 100% of proceeds from past customs to Minnesota Freedom Fund, Black Visions Collective, The Massachusetts Bail Fund, National Bail Out, among others.

Lauren Buehner

Lauren is a rising junior at Columbia University, majoring in Political Science and Human Rights with a specialization in International Relations. She plans to apply to a dual-degree program at SIPA to earn a Master of International Affairs in her 5th year, and after that plans to go to law school.

Outside of academics, Lauren is extremely involved in campus life. She is Vice President of the Columbia University Black Pre-Professional Society, and a member of both the Student Leadership Advisory Council and the Columbia University International Relations Council and Association.

In quarantine, she has been splitting her time between an internship with The HALO Trust, and building an online civic education resource.
